This project investigates how to automate the workflow of noun phrase identification in the tagged corpora of English, Chinese, and Korean to provide data for relevant linguistic studies. It is found that Chinese phrases in the form 'V+N1+de+N2' are especially difficult to correctly recognize because of their structural ambiguities, which leads to the project 'Mandarin Chinese ‘V+N1+de+N2’ Structure Disambiguation in Syntactic Parsing'.
