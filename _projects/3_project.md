The efficiency of neural dependency parsers often depends on the domain of corpus they are trained on. This projects assesses the efficiency of Chinese neural constituency parsers and investigates how to improve their performance. Surprisingly, we find that augmenting parsers with pre-trained contextualised word embeddings even hurts parsers' generalisability in some of our datasets. We developed a simple rule-based algorithm using less than 10 lines of code and reduced parsers' error rate by more than 30%.
